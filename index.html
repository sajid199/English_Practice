📖 Past Tense (भूतकाल)

🏃‍♀️ Simple Past
Subject + V2 (past form of verb)
Example: She went to market.
Hindi: वह बाजार गई थी।
📖 Past Continuous
Subject + was/were + V-ing
Example: I was reading a book.
Hindi: मैं किताब पढ़ रहा था।
✅ Past Perfect
Subject + had + V3
Example: He had left before I came.
Hindi: मैं आने से पहले वह जा चुका था।
⏰ Past Perfect Continuous
Subject + had + been + V-ing
Example: They had been working all day.
Hindi: वे पूरा दिन काम कर रहे थे।

🔮 Future Tense (भविष्यत काल)

🔮 Simple Future
Subject + will + V1
Example: She will come tomorrow.
Hindi: वह कल आएगी।
✈️ Future Continuous
Subject + will be + V-ing
Example: I will be traveling next week.
Hindi: मैं अगले सप्ताह यात्रा कर रहा हूँ।
🎯 Future Perfect
Subject + will have + V3
Example: They will have completed the work by evening.
Hindi: वे शाम तक काम पूरा कर चुके होंगे।
📅 Future Perfect Continuous
Subject + will have been + V-ing
Example: By next month, he will have been working here for a year.
Hindi: अगले महीने तक, वह यहाँ एक साल से काम कर रहा होगा।
