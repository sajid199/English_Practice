English Tenses Rules with Hindi Examples

1. Present Tense (वर्तमान काल)

Type Structure Example (English) Example (Hindi → English)
Simple Present Subject + V1 (base verb) / V1+s/es She reads books. वह किताबें पढ़ती है।
Present Continuous Subject + is/am/are + V-ing He is playing cricket. वह क्रिकेट खेल रहा है।
Present Perfect Subject + has/have + V3 (past participle) I have finished my work. मैंने अपना काम पूरा कर लिया है।
Present Perfect Continuous Subject + has/have + been + V-ing They have been studying since morning. वे सुबह से पढ़ाई कर रहे हैं।

2. Past Tense (भूतकाल)

Type Structure Example (English) Example (Hindi → English)
Simple Past Subject + V2 (past form of verb) She went to market. वह बाजार गई थी।
Past Continuous Subject + was/were + V-ing I was reading a book. मैं किताब पढ़ रहा था।
Past Perfect Subject + had + V3 He had left before I came. मैं आने से पहले वह जा चुका था।
Past Perfect Continuous Subject + had + been + V-ing They had been working all day. वे पूरा दिन काम कर रहे थे।

3. Future Tense (भविष्यत काल)

Type Structure Example (English) Example (Hindi → English)
Simple Future Subject + will + V1 She will come tomorrow. वह कल आएगी।
Future Continuous Subject + will be + V-ing I will be traveling next week. मैं अगले सप्ताह यात्रा कर रहा हूँ।
Future Perfect Subject + will have + V3 They will have completed the work by evening. वे शाम तक काम पूरा कर चुके होंगे।
Future Perfect Continuous Subject + will have been + V-ing By next month, he will have been working here for a year. अगले महीने तक, वह यहाँ एक साल से काम कर रहा होगा।
